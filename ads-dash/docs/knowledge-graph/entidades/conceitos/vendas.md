# Vendas

## Tipo

**Conceito**  (concept)

## Descricao

- Vendas refers to sales data indicating the volume of sales resulting from marketing efforts or campaigns.
- Vendas refers to services that have been effectively closed after service delivery and represents the final step in the sales funnel.
- Vendas represents the number of sales completed through the funnel.
- A variable representing the number of sales made on a given day.
- A metric representing approved sales per day, with a current value of 25 and it is enabled.
- The number of sales resulting from the campaigns, displayed in the CampaignTable.
- Sales metrics that reflect the number of transactions or items sold, which can influence the overall financial performance of a company.
