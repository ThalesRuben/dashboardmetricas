# CTR

## Tipo

**Dado**  (data)

## Descricao

- Click-through rate, a metric that reflects the percentage of viewers that clicked on a piece of content compared to those who viewed it.>
- A metric used to measure the effectiveness of advertising, specifically the click-through rate which indicates the percentage of users who clicked on an ad compared to those who viewed it.
- CTR, or Click-Through Rate, is a metric used to measure how often people click on an advertisement or post compared to how often it's seen.
- The Click-Through Rate of the campaigns expressed as a percentage in the CampaignTable.
