# Competitors

## Tipo

**Diverso**  (other)

## Descricao

- Competitors refer to various entities that operate in the same industry or market as the user, offering essential insights for analysis and comparison. In the context of performance benchmarking, these competitors include rival brands and reference profiles that users wish to monitor through specific applications. They are typically represented in a section identified as 'CONCORRENTES,' which may incorporate a unique color and number (08) within the visual identity framework, suggesting a structured presentation of these entities.

The term encompasses a comprehensive list of rival entities against which the main entity is compared. This collection is crucial for analytical purposes and is highlighted in components such as ValidatedContent, where the content of these competitors is actively analyzed. Additionally, there is the capability to filter this group, allowing users to focus on particular aspects or attributes relevant to their industry.

Moreover, competitors serve as external signals for reputation analysis, providing valuable information regarding performance benchmarking within the specified market. They integrate a range of activities and characteristics that are significant for tracking and analytics, thereby shaping the strategic landscape in which the user operates. Overall, the concept of Competitors captures the essence of competitive entities contributing to market dynamics and user decision-making processes.
