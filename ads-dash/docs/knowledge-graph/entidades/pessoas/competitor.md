# Competitor

## Tipo

**Pessoa**  (person)

## Descricao

- A general term referring to entities that compete in the same market or industry as the user.
- A general term for an individual or entity engaged in competition within a specific market or industry, commonly related to business or branding contexts, such as hair salons and beauty services.
- An individual competitor whose metrics and information are displayed in the CompetitorCard.
